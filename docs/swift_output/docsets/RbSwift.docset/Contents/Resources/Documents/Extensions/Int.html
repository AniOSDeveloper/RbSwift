  • bitLength

    Returns the number of bits of the value of Integer. the number of bits means that the bit position of the highest bit which is different to the sign bit. (The bit position of the bit 2**n is n+1.) If there is no such bit (zero or minus one), zero is returned.

    (-(2 ** 12) - 1).bitLength      #=> 13
(-(2 ** 12)).bitLength          #=> 12
(-(2 ** 12) + 1).bitLength      #=> 12
(-0x101).bitLength              #=> 9
(-0x100).bitLength              #=> 8
(-0xff).bitLength               #=> 8
(-2).bitLength                  #=> 1
(-1).bitLength                  #=> 0
0.bitLength                     #=> 0
1.bitLength                     #=> 1
0xff.bitLength                  #=> 8
0x100.bitLength                 #=> 9
(2 ** 12 - 1).bitLength         #=> 12
(2 ** 12).bitLength             #=> 13
(2 ** 12 + 1).bitLength         #=> 13
